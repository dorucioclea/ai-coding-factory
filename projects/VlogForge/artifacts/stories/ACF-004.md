# ACF-004: Analytics Dashboard

**Status**: Ready
**Priority**: P1 - Must Have (MVP)
**Epic**: Analytics
**Estimate**: L

## User Story

As a **creator**, I want to **view my analytics dashboard** so that **I understand my performance across all connected platforms**.

## Acceptance Criteria

### AC1: Overview Metrics
- **Given** a creator with at least one connected platform
- **When** they view the dashboard
- **Then** they see total followers, total views, and engagement rate across all platforms

### AC2: Trend Visualization
- **Given** a creator viewing the dashboard
- **When** they select a time period (7/30/90 days)
- **Then** they see line charts showing follower growth, views, and engagement over time

### AC3: Platform Breakdown
- **Given** a creator with multiple connected platforms
- **When** they view the dashboard
- **Then** they see metrics broken down by platform with comparison capabilities

### AC4: Top Content
- **Given** a creator with synced content data
- **When** they view the dashboard
- **Then** they see their top 10 performing pieces of content by views/engagement

### AC5: Growth Metrics
- **Given** a creator viewing analytics
- **When** they compare current period to previous period
- **Then** they see percentage change indicators (up/down arrows with percentages)

## Technical Notes

- Aggregate metrics calculated via background job (every 6 hours)
- Store daily snapshots for historical trending
- Use Recharts for visualization components
- Cache dashboard data in Redis (15 minute TTL)
